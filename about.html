ABOUT 30 MFF

30 Minute Film Festival is a film festival held in NYU Abu Dhabi, to bring out creativity from minds of aspiring filmmakers and artists. Teams are given 30 minutes to produce a short film about topic of their choice. 2018 is the fourth year of the festival, and has received good reception throughout the years.

ABOUT re:Bilie Jean

re:Billie Jean is a project created for NYUAD 30 Minute Film Festival 2018. Inspired by Michael Jackson's all-time legendary masterpiece Billie Jean , it strives to recreate the storyline through hand performances that reflect its lyrics.
